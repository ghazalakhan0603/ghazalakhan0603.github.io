STEAM Activity

  1. Science: This involves the exploration and understanding of the natural world. It enco urages critical thinking, observation, experimentation, and the development of scientific knowledge and skills.
  2. Technology: Focuses on the use of tools and resources to solve problems. It includes digital technologies, programming, and understanding how various technologies work.
  3. Engineering: Involves the application of scientific and mathematical principles to design and create solutions to real-world problems. It encourages problem-solving, creativity, and innovation.
  4. Arts: Emphasizes creativity, expression, and aesthetics. Integrating arts into STEM fosters a more holistic approach to education, encouraging students to think creatively and apply artistic principles to their work.
  5. Mathematics: Provides the fundamental language for understanding and describing the world. It includes logical reasoning, problem-solving, and mathematical modeling.
